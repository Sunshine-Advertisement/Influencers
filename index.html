    HOW WE

    WORK

    1. IDEAS
      We'll provide you with the campaign brief, including our brand's key message and in some cases we will also provide Ideas.
    2. SCRIPT
      You and our team will work on to develop a concept and script for the Reel.
    3. SHOOT & EDIT
      Once the concept is approved by our clients, you'll proceed with shooting and editing the video, and under some circumstances we will also get involve in shooting and editing of video.
    4. FEEDBACK
      We'll work closely with you to provide feedback and ensure the final product aligns with our client's vision.
    5. LAUNCH
      Upon completion, Full Transition of agreed fees will be provided. As the payment is done, the reel will be uploded on your Handle and our client's desired accounts. You won't be having any rights on the reel.
